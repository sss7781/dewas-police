OUR Mission

Dewas Police will strive towards excellence in all aspects of policing with the help of modern technological inputs and community participation and achieve the targets through constant up gradation of resources, techniques and professional skills The purpose of Dewas Police is to uphold the law fairly and firmly. We, the police force of Ujjain, are committed to be compassionate, courteous and patient, to act without fear or favor. We have resolved to be professional, calm and restrained in the face of violence and apply only that force which is necessary to accomplish our lawful duty.
